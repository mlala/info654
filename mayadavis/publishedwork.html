Heaven, My Home Interview

Cheeseit: Small Rat, Strange City

I had the chance to sit down and talk to Attica Locke about her latest mystery novel, Heaven, My Home, the second book in the Highway 59 series and anticipated follow-up to Bluebird, Bluebird.

My debut digital comic about a rat named Cheeseit. Chester "Cheeseit" LaFromage is having a very ordinary day. As a bike courier, she pedals around the neighborhood, delivering packages to various clients. Her eccentric boss Ovaltine tasks her with a special delivery somewhere new, but she can hardly focus on her route when her friends keep blowing up her phone with all their problems.
